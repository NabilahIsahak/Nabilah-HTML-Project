EXPERIENCE


This picture reminds me of my experience and friends while participating in the go green program organized by Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Tinggi Port Dicksin with HRC company in 2017 where all activities carried out at that time were based on recycle materials processed into some games like bowling, kite, shirt demonstration using recycled materials and so on. At that time, almost all students participated in all the programs while my friends and I did not miss the opportunity to participate in one of the activities in the program, which is to create kites using recycled materials, which my group has 5 members. A few days before the competition my friends and I had made preparations to make the kites for use on the day of the competition, the time period given was 3 days and on the day of the program my friends and I had to bring the kites to school to be shown to many people. We got the second place for the competition and earned as much as RM 250.00, this has given joy to me and my friends who worked hard in preparing a kite as well as possible. This is the most valuable experience for me because with this program it can teach me to be more enthusiastic and creative in producing a game only using recycle materials.

MY EDUCATION🎓


Primary School

During my year 1 I was sent by my parents to port dickson national school located in the center of port dickson to get education and knowledge for 6 years starting from year 1 to year 6. In 2012, i sat for a big exam which is "Ujian Penilaian Sekolah Rendah" (UPSR) which is a compulsory examination to be taken by every student in year 6 where the results are quite encouraging at that time I was getting 2A 3B covering all major subjects namely the "Bahasa Melayu", (writing and understanding), English, Mathematics, and Science.

Secondary School

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Tinggi Port Dickson is a school that accommodates students aged 13 - 19 where this school prepares for PT3, SPM and STPM but I only sat for two exams which are PT3 (2015) and SPM (2017). during PT3 I have held a few subjects such as "Bahasa Melayu", English, "Sejarah", Mathematic, Science, "Pendidikan Islam", "Geografi" and "Kemahiran Hidup". In 2017, I have sat for the SPM examination, that examination must be taken by all students to pursue their studies at the university level and among the subjects I was sitting on the SPM were "Bahasa Melayu", English, "Pendidikan Islam", Mathematic, "Sejarah", Physics, Chemistry, and "Prinsip Akaun".
